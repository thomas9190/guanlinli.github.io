## About Me
I’m a PhD student focusing on **head‑mounted display (HMD) ergonomics**, **interaction techniques**, and **human–computer interaction**. My work explores how we can improve comfort, usability, and performance in immersive systems.

- Interaction in extended reality (XR)  
- Human–Computer Interaction
